Our idealogy

We are passionate about providing our customers with the best possible food experience, and we believe that starts with using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients available. Shop our selection today and taste the difference that farm-fresh, local, and pesticide-free food can make! We're a farm-to-people company that is dedicated to providing fresh, delicious meals made with:

  • locally-sourced
  • organic
  • sustainable
  • seasonal

Our products changes seasonally to ensure that we're always using the best and most delicious ingredients. We believe that eating seasonally not only tastes better, but it's also better for the environment and for your health. That's why we work closely with local farmers to source the freshest produce and meat possible..

What is the best way to store my food?

The best way to store your food is in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. If you have a refrigerator, you can store your food in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. If you don't have a refrigerator, you can store your food in the freezer for up to 6 months.
